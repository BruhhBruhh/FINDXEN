Our Purpose

XEN Crypto is built for anyone to join, connecting people through a fair, community-driven token.

The Fair Crypto Foundation designed it to echo blockchain’s roots—decentralized, transparent, and self-owned.

How It Works

XEN starts with no supply; you mint it using an Ethereum wallet under a Proof of Participation model.

Rewards shrink as participation grows, shifting it from inflationary to scarcer over time.

Straightforward Design

Running on Ethereum as an ERC-20 token, XEN’s open-source code has no central control or pre-mints.

You connect a wallet, set a wait period, and mint it—that’s the deal.

Minting & Staking

Rewards follow Ru = log₂(cRG - cRu) × T × AMP(tsG) × (1 + EAA(cRu)), based on your rank, wait days (T), and declining amplifiers.

Stake for 1–1,000 days with APY from 20% down to 2%, but claim late and lose up to 99%.

Community Power

XEN gains value as more people mint and trade it on platforms like Uniswap.

It’s yours to use, share, or hold, driven by collective adoption.
