Minting XEN

Process: Claim a CryptoRank (\( cR_u \)) via `claimRank(term)` (1 to freeMintTermLimit days). Post-term, claim via `claimMintReward()` within 7 days or face penalties (0% day 0, 99% day 7+) (p. 7-8, 13).

Formula: \( R_u = \log_2(cR_G - cR_u) * T * AMP(t s_G) * (1 + EAA(cR_u)) \). FreeMintTerm starts at 100 days, increasing as \( 100 + \log_2(cR_G) * 15 \) after 5,000 participants (p. 7).

Staking XEN

Process: Stake via `stake(amount, term)` (1-1,000 days). APY starts at 20%, drops 1% every 90 days to 2%. Rewards: \( R_u = \frac{S_u * APY * \tau}{100 * 365} \). Early withdrawal returns stake only; post-maturity includes APY (p. 8-9, 14).

Technology

Blockchain: XEN runs on Ethereum mainnet as an ERC-20 token, leveraging its $570B peak market cap security (p. 10).

First Principles: Inspired by crypto-anarchism and Bitcoin’s P2P ethos, XEN resists centralization trends (e.g., CBDCs) with a trustless design (p. 11).

The Cause
